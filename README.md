# Seatmate — Releases

**Seatmate** is an offline study companion for Filipino students. Track your quizzes, subjects, grades, and notes while **Ella** — your seatmate — cheers you on in natural Taglish. No account, no internet, no ads. Your data never leaves your phone.

## Installing

Seatmate isn't on the Play Store yet, so Android will ask you to allow installing apps from your browser the first time — that's standard for directly-distributed apps.

1. Download the APK above on your Android phone.
2. Open it. If prompted, tap **Settings → Allow from this source**.
3. Tap **Install**. Tapos! Ella's waiting for you. 👋

**Requirements:** Android 7.0 or newer.

## Privacy

Everything is stored locally on your device — no account, no cloud, no analytics, zero data uploaded.
